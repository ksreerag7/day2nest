Kerala
God's Own Country

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is renowned for its serene backwaters, rich culture, and lush green landscapes. It is often referred to as "God's Own Country" due to its natural beauty, temples, and diverse cultural heritage.

Famous Attractions:

  • Alleppey Backwaters

  • Munnar Hill Station
    •
  • Fort Kochi
    •
  • Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary
    •
  • Kovalam Beach
    •

Kerala is also famous for its festivals, Ayurvedic treatments, and its culinary delights, including traditional Kerala Sadya.
